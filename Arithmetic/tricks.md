## **1. Fast Addition Tricks**

### **A. Break Down Numbers**
Break numbers into easier parts (e.g., tens and units) and add them separately.

**Example:**
`47 + 28`
Б†’ `40 + 20 = 60`
Б†’ `7 + 8 = 15`
Б†’ **Total: 60 + 15 = 75**

### **B. Compensation Method**
Adjust one number to make the addition easier, then compensate.

**Example:**
`58 + 29`
Б†’ `58 + 30 = 88`
Б†’ Subtract the extra `1` Б†’ **87**

### **C. Use Round Numbers**
Round numbers to the nearest ten, then adjust.

**Example:**
`36 + 48`
Б†’ `40 + 50 = 90`
Б†’ Subtract `4 + 2 = 6` Б†’ **84**

### **A. Multiply by 10, 100, etc.**

**Example:**

## **3. Fast Division Tricks**

### **A. Divide by 5**
Multiply by 2 and divide by 10.

**Example:**
`120 ц· 5`
Б†’ `120 ц— 2 = 240`
Б†’ `240 ц· 10 = 24`

---

### **B. Divide by 10, 100, etc.**
Move the decimal point left.

**Example:**
`230 ц· 10 = 23.0`
`230 ц· 100 = 2.30`

---

### **C. Divide by 9**
Use the **digit sum method** for divisibility and quick division.

**Example:**
`135 ц· 9`
Б†’ Sum of digits: `1 + 3 + 5 = 9` (divisible by 9)
Б†’ `135 ц· 9 = 15`

---

### **D. Divide Using Multiplication**
Find the reciprocal and multiply.

**Example:**
`100 ц· 4`
Б†’ `100 ц— 0.25 = 25`

---

### **E. Long Division Shortcut**
Break the divisor into easier parts.

**Example:**
`144 ц· 12`
Б†’ `12 ц— 10 = 120`
Б†’ `144 Бқ“ 120 = 24`
Б†’ `12 ц— 2 = 24`
Б†’ **Total: 10 + 2 = 12**
