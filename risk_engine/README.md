# Risk Engine

A Python-based risk management and portfolio analysis toolkit for calculating risk metrics, backtesting strategies, and optimizing portfolios.

---

## **Overview**

The **Risk Engine** is a modular Python library designed for quantitative finance applications. It provides tools for:
- Calculating risk metrics like **Value at Risk (VaR)** and **Conditional Value at Risk (CVaR)**.
- Managing and optimizing **portfolios**.
- Defining and **backtesting trading strategies**.
- Loading and preprocessing financial data.

---

## **Features**

- **Risk Metrics**: Calculate VaR, CVaR, and other risk measures.
- **Portfolio Management**: Optimize asset allocations and calculate portfolio returns and volatility.
- **Strategy Backtesting**: Define, simulate, and evaluate trading strategies.
- **Data Handling**: Load and preprocess financial datasets.
- **Modular Design**: Easy to extend and customize for specific use cases.
